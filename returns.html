Returns & Exchanges

Our hassle-free return policy

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your order, we offer a straightforward return policy.

Return Window

14 Days

From the date of delivery

Condition

Unworn & Tagged

Original packaging required

How to Return

  1. Contact Us: Email us at hello@kamelkross.com with your order number and reason for return.
  2. Get Approval: We'll respond within 24-48 hours with return instructions and a return authorization number.
  3. Pack It Up: Place the item(s) in the original packaging with all tags attached.
  4. Ship It: Send the package to the address provided in your return authorization email.
  5. Get Refunded: Once we receive and inspect the item, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Exchange Policy

Need a different size or color? We're happy to help with exchanges.

  • Exchanges are subject to stock availability
  • If the new item has a different price, we'll adjust accordingly
  • Follow the same process as returns, but specify you want an exchange

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items without original tags
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Items returned after the 14-day window

Refund Information

  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer (unless the item was defective or we made an error)

Damaged or Defective Items?

If you received a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately with photos of the issue. We'll make it right!
