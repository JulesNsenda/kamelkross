Shipping Information

Everything you need to know about delivery

Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to all major cities and towns across South Africa. Our delivery network covers:

  • Gauteng (Johannesburg, Pretoria, etc.)
  • Western Cape (Cape Town, Stellenbosch, etc.)
  • KwaZulu-Natal (Durban, Pietermaritzburg, etc.)
  • Eastern Cape (Port Elizabeth, East London, etc.)
  • Free State, Mpumalanga, Limpopo, North West, Northern Cape

Delivery Times

Major Cities

2-4 Business Days

Johannesburg, Cape Town, Durban, Pretoria

Other Areas

4-7 Business Days

All other towns and rural areas

Please note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary during peak periods or due to unforeseen circumstances.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on your location and order size. The exact shipping fee will be displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an email with your tracking number and a link to track your package. You can use this to monitor your delivery status in real-time.
